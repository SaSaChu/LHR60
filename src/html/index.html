Ultra High Speed HDMI Cable

The ROG HDMI 2.1 cable offers 48Gbps bandwidth — 2.6X the bandwidth of HDMI 2.0 — to support up to 8K (7680 x 4320) uncompressed video and offer reliable, high-speed transmission with no signal loss. The cable also supports 4K@144Hz visuals, variable refresh rate (VRR), and HDMI Quick Frame Transport (QFT) to reduce latency.

Via HDMI VRR

HDMI VRR ensures a premium gaming experience by eliminating stuttering and screen tearing. Featuring QFT for ultra-low latency and minimal input lag, it delivers seamless performance and smooth visuals.
